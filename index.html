Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 February 2026

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers is an educational app designed for children to help them learn alphabets (A–Z), counting (1–10), and basic math questions in a simple and fun way.

Information Collection and Use

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

  • No user registration or login required
  • No personal data is requested
  • No location, contacts, or device information is accessed
  • The app works completely offline

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children. Since the app does not collect any data, there is no risk of children’s data misuse.

Educational Content

The app provides learning content including:

  • Alphabets A–Z with two images per letter
  • Counting from 1 to 10
  • Basic math questions (up to 10 simple questions)

Third-Party Resources

The app uses third-party resources only for visual and audio content:

  • Icons: Icons are sourced from Flaticon and are created by different authors under Flaticon’s license.
  • Background Music: Background music is sourced from the YouTube Audio Library and is royalty-free.

These third-party resources do not collect or process any user data through the app.

Advertisements

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers does not show any advertisements.

Data Security

We value user privacy. Since no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure or misuse.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
