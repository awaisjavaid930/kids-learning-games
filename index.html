Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 February 2026

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers is an educational application created to help children learn alphabets (A–Z), counting (1–10), and basic math in a fun and simple way.

Important: We respect children’s privacy. This app does not require user registration and is designed in compliance with Google Play’s Families Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers does not collect or store personal information from users.

  • No sign-up or login required
  • No personal details are requested
  • No access to contacts, camera, or storage
  • No location tracking
  • Most content works offline

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children.

Since the app itself does not collect data, there is minimal risk of data misuse.

Educational Content

The app includes the following learning features:

  • Alphabets A–Z with visual examples
  • Counting numbers from 1 to 10
  • Simple and basic math questions

Third-Party Resources

The app uses limited third-party resources only for design and sound:

  • Icons: Provided by Flaticon under their respective licenses.
  • Audio: Background music sourced from the YouTube Audio Library and used under royalty-free terms.

Advertisements

Kids Learning Games – ABC & Numbers shows advertisements.

Ads are provided by trusted third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect limited, non-personal, and anonymous information (such as device type or ad performance data) to display appropriate ads.

All advertisements comply with Google Play Families Policy and are designed to be suitable for children.

Data Security

We take privacy seriously. As the app does not store personal data, there is no database or user information at risk.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
